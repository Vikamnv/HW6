About Series

Full Description

The series follows the reign of Sultan Suleiman. At the age of 26, Suleiman learns that his father has died and that his own reign is about to begin. He is determined to build an empire more powerful than that of Alexander the Great and make it invincible. Over the course of his 46-year reign, his fame as the greatest warrior and ruler of his time will spread to both East and West. With his companion Pargalı İbrahim, Suleiman will achieve great victories, making his name known in the Muslim World. Instead we have arriving from a slave ship Alexandra Lisowska, a Ukrainian lady who was kidnapped at the age of 17 before marrying her fiancé and taken to the Muslim lands where she enters the harem and once there she will attract the attention of Suleiman the Magnificent, who will soon begin an intimate relationship with her.
